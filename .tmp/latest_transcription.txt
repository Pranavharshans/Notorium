 Welcome to level five of 100 paradoxes. This one is called the crocodile paradox and it's all about logic, predictions, and a very tricky dilemma. Imagine a cunning crocodile lurking by the riverbank. One day it grabs a child. The desperate parent begs for the child's return. The crocodile, being unusually logical, makes a deal. It says, I will return your child if and only if you correctly predict what I will do next. Now, the parent thinks carefully and makes a prediction. You will not return my child. Now, the crocodile is stuck in a paradox. If the prediction is correct, then the crocodile must return the child since that was the deal.
